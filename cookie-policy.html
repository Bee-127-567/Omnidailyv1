Cookie Policy

Last updated: January 6, 2026

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

Similar technologies include web beacons, pixels, and local storage. For the purposes of this policy, all these technologies are referred to as "cookies."

2. How We Use Cookies

Omni.Daily uses cookies for the following purposes:

Type of Cookie Purpose Examples
Essential Cookies Required for the website to function properly. These cannot be disabled. Session management, security, load balancing
Functional Cookies Remember your preferences and settings to enhance your experience. Language preferences, tool settings, theme preferences
Analytics Cookies Help us understand how visitors interact with our website. Page views, tool usage, visitor sources
Advertising Cookies Used by our advertising partners to show relevant ads. Google AdSense cookies for personalized ads

3. Detailed Cookie Information

3.1 Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function and cannot be switched off.

  • session_id: Maintains your session across pages
  • security_token: Protects against cross-site request forgery
  • consent_status: Remembers your cookie consent choice

3.2 Analytics Cookies

We use privacy-focused analytics that minimize data collection:

  • _ga: Google Analytics - Distinguishes users (expires: 2 years)
  • _gid: Google Analytics - Distinguishes users (expires: 24 hours)
  • _gat: Google Analytics - Throttles request rate (expires: 1 minute)
  • plausible_ignore: Plausible Analytics - Opt-out cookie

3.3 Advertising Cookies

Third-party advertising cookies (managed by Google AdSense):

  • _gads / _gac: Google AdSense campaign tracking
  • IDE: Google DoubleClick - Behavioral advertising
  • test_cookie: Google DoubleClick - Checks if cookies are enabled

For more information about Google's cookies, visit Google's Cookie Policy.

Cookie Preferences

You can manage your cookie preferences below. Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are required for the website to function.

4. How to Control Cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish:

4.1 Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. Here's how to manage cookies in popular browsers:

  • Google Chrome: Settings ‚Üí Privacy and security ‚Üí Cookies and other site data
  • Mozilla Firefox: Options ‚Üí Privacy & Security ‚Üí Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Cookies and website data
  • Microsoft Edge: Settings ‚Üí Cookies and site permissions ‚Üí Cookies and data stored

4.2 Do Not Track

Our website respects the "Do Not Track" browser setting. When this setting is enabled, we will disable all non-essential cookies.

4.3 Third-Party Opt-Out

For advertising cookies, you can opt out through:

  • Google Ads Settings
  • Digital Advertising Alliance
  • Your Online Choices (EU)
