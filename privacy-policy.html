Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 6, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Omni.Daily ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information when you visit our website at omnidaily.netlify.app (the "Site") and use our online tools and services.

2. Information We Collect

We operate with a privacy-first approach. Unlike many websites, we intentionally minimize data collection:

2.1 Data You Provide

  • Tool Input Data: When you use our tools (calculators, converters, etc.), the data you input is processed locally in your browser. We do not store or transmit this data to our servers.
  • Contact Information: If you contact us via email, we collect your email address and the content of your message.

2.2 Automatically Collected Data

  • Usage Data: We use anonymized analytics to understand how our tools are used (pages visited, tool usage frequency) without identifying individual users.
  • Technical Data: Browser type, device type, operating system, and approximate location (country-level only).
  • Cookies: We use essential cookies for site functionality. See our Cookie Policy for details.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and maintain our tools and services
  • To improve and optimize user experience
  • To respond to your inquiries and provide support
  • To analyze usage patterns and improve tool functionality
  • To detect, prevent, and address technical issues

4. Data Storage and Security

Local Processing: All tool calculations and conversions happen locally in your browser using JavaScript. Your input data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export or share results.

No User Accounts: We don't require user registration, so we don't store usernames, passwords, or personal profiles.

Analytics Data: Usage statistics are aggregated and anonymized, making it impossible to identify individual users.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google AdSense: Displays advertisements. Google may collect data for personalized ads. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.
  • Netlify: Our hosting provider. They may collect server logs for security and maintenance.
  • Analytics Services: We use privacy-focused analytics that respect Do Not Track requests.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights regarding your personal information:

  • Right to access your data
  • Right to correct inaccurate data
  • Right to delete your data
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@omnidaily.com.

7. Children's Privacy

Our Site is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date. You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

9. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

  • Email: privacy@omnidaily.com
  • Contact Form: Visit our Contact page

This Privacy Policy was last updated on January 6, 2026.
